## 🎯 Core Goals
- Demystify what "Custom GPTs" actually are under the hood.
- Learn how to package your own AI workflows.

:::callout-tldr
Whenever you use a "Custom GPT" for cooking recipes or a specific Claude "Project", you aren't using a different AI model. You're just using the standard model with a very long, custom System Prompt permanently glued to the top!
:::

## 📝 Key Concepts

- **Pre-Packaged Rules:** A Custom Space is simply a saved environment. Instead of typing out *"Act as a French chef and only give me vegan recipes"* every single time you open a chat, you save it as a System Prompt. 
- **Knowledge Bases:** These spaces also allow you to upload files (like PDF menus). The system automatically attaches relevant parts of these files to your "receipt tape" (Context Window) whenever you ask a question.
- **Sharing the Hat:** When someone sells or shares a "Custom GPT", they are essentially just sharing the specific "hat" (System Prompt + Files) they created for the AI to wear.

:::callout-dyk
Creating your own custom workspace is the fastest way to get value out of AI. Instead of typing long prompts, you build the prompt once and just use the chat for quick questions!
:::
