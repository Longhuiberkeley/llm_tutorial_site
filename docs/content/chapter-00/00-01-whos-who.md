# Page 0.1: Who's Who in LLMs

## The Big Players in AI

### 🏢 Closed Source Leaders

| Company | Flagship Models | Known For |
|---------|-----------------|-----------|
| **Anthropic** | Claude 3/4 (Opus, Sonnet, Haiku) | AI safety, long context, thoughtful responses |
| **OpenAI** | GPT-4o, GPT-4.1, o1 | First mover, huge ecosystem, plugins |
| **Google** | Gemini 2.0/3.0 | Multimodal native, integrated with Google products |
| **Meta** | Llama (open weights) | Open-sourcing powerful models |
| **xAI** | Grok | Real-time knowledge, Twitter integration |

### 🎯 Quick Takeaways

- **Anthropic (Claude)** → Safety-focused, great for writing and analysis
- **OpenAI (GPT)** → Ecosystem leader, most integrations available
- **Google (Gemini)** → Best at multimodal (images, video, code)
- **Meta (Llama)** → Open weights, runs anywhere, customizable
- **xAI (Grok)** → Real-time info, more "uncensored"

### 💡 Why This Matters

- Different models excel at different tasks
- You might use Claude for writing, GPT for coding, Gemini for images
- Understanding who makes what helps you choose the right tool

### 🤔 Things to Consider

- **API access** varies by provider
- **Pricing** differs significantly (we'll cover this next)
- **Safety filters** vary (more on this in page 0.6)
