# Page 0.2: Model Sizes & Costs

## Model Tiers Explained

### 🎖️ The Three Tiers (Using Claude as Example)

| Tier | Model | Best For | Cost | Speed |
|------|-------|----------|------|-------|
| **Flagship** | Opus 4.6 | Complex reasoning, writing, analysis | $$$$ | 🐢 |
| **Balanced** | Sonnet 4.6 | Most tasks, coding, everyday use | $$ | 🚶 |
| **Fast/Cheap** | Haiku 4.5 | Simple tasks, high volume, chatbots | $ | 🚀 |

### 📊 Comparative Examples

| Provider | Flagship | Balanced | Fast |
|----------|----------|----------|------|
| **Anthropic** | Opus 4.6 | Sonnet 4.6 | Haiku 4.5 |
| **OpenAI** | GPT-4.1 | GPT-4o | GPT-4o-mini |
| **Google** | Gemini 3.0 Pro | Gemini 2.5 | Gemini 1.5 Flash |

### 🎯 When to Use What

**Use Flagship (Opus/GPT-4.1) for:**
- Complex reasoning and analysis
- Important business documents
- Creative writing that needs nuance
- When accuracy is critical

**Use Balanced (Sonnet/GPT-4o) for:**
- Most coding tasks
- Everyday business use
- Content generation
- Best bang for your buck

**Use Fast/Cheap (Haiku/Mini) for:**
- Simple classification
- High-volume tasks
- Chatbots
- When speed matters more than quality

### 💰 Cost Intuition

- **Opus**: ~15x more expensive than Haiku
- **Sonnet**: ~3x more expensive than Haiku
- **Haiku**: Fastest, cheapest, still very capable

**Rule of thumb:** Start with Balanced (Sonnet), upgrade to Flagship only if needed.

### ⚡ Speed Differences

- **Haiku/Mini**: Responses in ~1 second
- **Sonnet/GPT-4o**: Responses in ~2-3 seconds
- **Opus/GPT-4.1**: Responses in ~5-10 seconds
