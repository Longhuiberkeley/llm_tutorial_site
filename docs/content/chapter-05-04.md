## 🎯 Core Goals
- Re-calibrate expectations for AI output.
- Learn the "80/20" rule of AI collaboration.

:::callout-tldr
Never copy-paste AI text directly into a final email or document. The AI's job is to conquer the blank page and give you a rough 1st draft. Your job is to make it sound human.
:::

## 📝 Key Concepts

- **The 80/20 Rule:** AI is spectacular at doing the first 80% of the work in 20% of the time (outlining, drafting, structuring). The last 20% (editing, tone-matching, fact-checking) is where you add the human value.
- **The "AI Voice":** Words like *delve, testament, tapestry, robust, and intricate* are statistically over-represented in AI training data. If you don't edit the draft, people will instantly know an AI wrote it.
- **Iterative Steering:** Don't just accept the first draft. Tell the AI *"Make the second paragraph punchier"* or *"Change the tone to be more casual."* Steer the machine!

:::callout-dyk
Studies show that employees who use AI to generate a first draft and then heavily edit it themselves produce significantly higher quality work than those who just use the raw AI output!
:::
