2.6 RLHF & the Reasoning Era

How reinforcement learning shapes today's AI, and the concept of thinking models.

🎯 Core Goals

  • Explain how Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF) works.
  • Introduce the concept of "thinking" tokens in modern models.
Modern models are trained using human feedback to prefer good answers over bad ones. The newest models even spend extra tokens "thinking" before they speak, improving their accuracy dramatically.

📝 Key Concepts

The RLHF Loop

  • Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF) is how we get models to align with human values.
  • The Loop: The model generates a few different responses → Humans rank which one is best → The model adjusts its internal math to prefer generating answers like the winning one.

The Reasoning Era

  • Modern models (like Claude 3.5+, GPT-o1, or specialized Gemini models) can be trained to "think" before answering.
  • Thinking Tokens: Instead of blurting out the first word that comes to mind, the model writes out its reasoning step-by-step behind the scenes before giving a final answer.
  • The Analogy: It's like a student showing their work on a math test. The process of writing out the steps helps them arrive at the right answer.
Wrong Math Still Helps! Researchers have found that even when an LLM's reasoning chain contains errors, the simple act of spending more tokens on reasoning improves final accuracy. It's like how scribbling on scratch paper helps you solve a math problem, even if your scribbles aren't perfectly organized.
