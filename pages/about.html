About This Tutorial

An interactive, concept-focused journey to understand Large Language Models — no technical background required.

🎯 What is this?

This is a free, interactive tutorial that teaches you how Large Language Models (like ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini) actually work. We break down complex concepts into bite-sized, interactive lessons that anyone can understand.

👥 Who is this for?

  • • Business professionals who want to understand AI to make better decisions
  • • Curious learners who want to peek under the hood of LLMs
  • • Anyone who uses ChatGPT but wonders "how does it actually do that?"

⚡ How does it work?

Each chapter contains short pages with:

  • • Clear explanations with real-world examples
  • • Interactive demos that let you see concepts in action
